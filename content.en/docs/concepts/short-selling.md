# Pool

## Overview

Each LAMM stores pooled reserves of two assets (`token0` and `token1`), corresponding to the two assets of the linked AMM pool. All margin trades are executed directly against the reserves of that AMM pool. Prices are set automatically using the constant product (`x*y=k`) market maker mechanism provided by the AMM.

Reserves are pooled by a network of liquidity providers who supply the system with tokens in exchange for a proportional share of transaction fees.
